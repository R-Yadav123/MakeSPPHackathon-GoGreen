    Greener Living

    How can we make the world greener?

    There are many ways we can make the world greener!

    We can:
    1. Save electricity.
    -By turning off lights when not in use.
    -Unplugging chargers from their outlets when not in use.
    2. Reduce the amount of fuel usage.
    -By riding a bike or walking when your destination is not far.
    3. Reduce, Reuse & Recycle!
    -Reduce the amount of plastic.(Example: Instead of plastic bags use paper bags or a disposable bag)
    -Reuse certain items. (Example: Reuse disposable grocery bags.)
    -Recycle certain items. (Example: Recycle water bottles and milk cartons!)
